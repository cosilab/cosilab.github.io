The Cooperative Systems and Intelligence (CoSI) lab is a research group in the National University of Singapore (NUS) Department of Computer Science, led by Assistant Professor [Tan Zhi Xuan](https://ztangent.github.io). Our focus is on **scaling cooperative intelligence** via rational, model-based AI engineering.

By reverse engineering the computational foundations of human cooperation, and using those insights to build reliable, coherent, and human-like cooperative systems, we aim to enable collective safety and flourishing in an increasingly automated future.
